Part A — Question 0: Playing with DeepFloyd

Text Prompts Used for Prompt Embeddings

To explore the behavior of the DeepFloyd diffusion model, I generated prompt embeddings for a diverse set of text prompts ranging from highly detailed, imaginative scenes to very simple object-level descriptions.

The prompts are intentionally ordered from more complex to more minimal. Complex prompts test the model’s ability to synthesize multiple concepts, spatial relationships, and artistic styles, while simpler prompts serve as controlled baselines for later experiments.

Additionally, several prompt pairs were designed specifically for use in later sections involving Hybrid Images and Visual Anagrams, where semantic blending or transformation-based ambiguity is required.
