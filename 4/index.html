Appendix

Notes & References

  • Alignment (2.2): two points per image → scale/rotate/center, crop overlap.
  • Stacks: Gaussian (size-preserving), Laplacian = Gk − Gk+1; residual last.
  • Blend rule: L = (1−M)·LA + M·LB, sum over levels.
  • Masks: step (vertical/horizontal) or local ellipse; white selects B.
