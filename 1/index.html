Part 2

Architectural Perspective Compression

The wide-angle view makes the lamp appear tall and distorted — especially the flag attached to it, which was originally rectangular but now looks like a parallelogram. With a longer lens, the space compresses, bringing background elements visually closer to the subject.

Part 3

Dolly Zoom

As I stepped back while zooming in with the camera, I didn’t notice any obvious differences between the individual shots. But when I combined them into a GIF, I saw that the background stretched dramatically as I moved away — while the subject (Labubu) remained roughly the same size.
